What does Green Mean?

The Green Party stands for real change—where "Green Means" acting for nature, the climate, our communities, and the future. Discover what Green Means for you and for everyone.

  • Green New Deal: Invest £40bn a year, creating jobs in renewables, efficiency, public transport, nature restoration, and constructions. Support green local enterprises and move the UK to net zero.
  • Fair Pay: £15 minimum wage for all ages, closing gender pay gap and ensuring living incomes keep up with the cost of living.
  • Fair Transition for Workers: Retraining and guaranteed careers guidance for workers in polluting industries.
  • Workers’ Rights Improvements: Restore union rights, repeal anti-union laws, introduce a Charter of Workers’ Rights, flexible and secure employment, work-life balance, move toward a four-day week, and real protections for gig and zero-hours contract workers.
  • End privatisation & invest: Restore the NHS, reverse marketisation, increase funding by £8bn/yr for staff and facilities.
  • Free personal care for all: Universal social care for adults with proper support for carers.
  • Mental health parity: Invest in community services and bring mental health to parity with physical health.
  • Accessible dentistry & GPs: Universal NHS dentistry and fast GP appointments for everyone.
  • Affordable housing: Build new eco‑friendly, affordable homes, rent controls, ban no‑fault evictions, and guarantee safe homes for all.
  • Social support: Increase benefits, scrap the two‑child cap, rebuild the social safety net.
  • Mental wellbeing, youth clubs: Fund youth services, mental health hubs, and inclusive community spaces.
  • Local solutions: Empower local communities with real power over planning and their futures.
  • Rapid net zero & renewables: End fossil fuel use, invest in wind, solar, and tidal power, and ban new oil and gas drilling.
  • Cheaper bills & energy security: Public/community renewables, home insulation, and fair prices to cut bills.
  • Nature-based climate solutions: Restore forests, peatlands, and river habitats to help tackle the climate crisis.
  • Cheaper, better public transport: Public trains and buses, lower fares, expanded networks, free bus travel for under‑18s.
  • Cycle & walk everywhere: Safe walking/cycling routes in every town and city.
  • Greener vehicles & less pollution: Accelerate electric vehicles and buses, strict pollution limits, charging stations nationwide.
  • Ban short flights: Domestic flights under three hours by train will be banned to cut carbon and boost rail.
  • Restoring Nature: Protect and restore at least 30% of UK land/sea for nature, rewilding areas, tree‑planting, and revived river habitats.
  • Clean air & water: Take water public, halt sewage dumping, guarantee the right to clean air and water for all.
  • Protect wildlife: Ban trophy hunting, restore nature reserves, and protect endangered species.
  • Support farmers: Promote organic farming, restore hedgerows, cut pesticides, guarantee fair food prices.
  • Better schools, more teachers: £8bn extra annual funding, smaller class sizes, more support staff.
  • Free lifelong learning: No tuition fees, restored grants, support for adult learning and retraining.
  • Creative, healthy schools: Scrap SATs/Ofsted, free healthy school meal for every child daily.
  • Inclusive education: Funded support for special needs and safe schools for all.
  • Proportional representation: Switch to fair voting and ensure every voice is heard.
  • Youth engagement: Lower voting age to 16, fund youth leadership and community projects.
  • Protect freedoms: Guarantee civil liberties, protest rights, freedom of expression.
  • Community power: Empower councils and regions to decide policy and budgets locally.
  • #FreePalestine – standing for international human rights and peace: The Green Party calls for a just, lasting peace for Palestine and Israel, upholding international law, supporting an end to occupation, and the right for Palestinians to live in freedom and dignity. We demand an immediate ceasefire, humanitarian assistance, diplomatic peace, and an end to UK arms sales to abusive regimes.
  • International peace & climate justice: Peace-led foreign policy, climate justice worldwide, and opposition to arms sales to oppressive regimes.
  • A future for everyone: Building a country and world that works for people, planet, and the generations to come. Fairness, wellbeing, and sustainability first.
